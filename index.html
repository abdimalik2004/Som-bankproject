We are Sombank

SOMBANK was launched on 15 July 2019 in Garowe, Somalia. It has a strong partnership with its sister Company of Tawakal Money Transfer. This means that it is in a position to tap into a large customer base which is already in place, and to also draw on the expertise its sister Company had amassed through its many years in the financial sector in Somalia.
SOMBANK had already made significant investment in fixed assets in Somalia in the form of Commercial Buildings developed in the Capital Mogadishu, Garowe, Galkacyo, Galdogob, Bosasso and many more such investments are planned.
We have an exceptional Leadership team, Operations team, as well as well-trained personnel that will deliver excellent services to our customers.
